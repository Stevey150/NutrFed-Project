About Us

At NutrFed, we’re on a mission to simplify healthy eating for college students. We know how challenging it can be to juggle a busy schedule, stick to a budget, and still eat nutritious meals. That’s why we created NutrFed—an app that transforms the ingredients you already have into quick, easy, and healthy recipes.

Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, NutrFed is here to help you nourish your body and mind without the stress.

Why Choose NutrFed?

  • Save Time: Stop wasting time searching for recipes or meal ideas. NutrFed does it for you.
  • Save Money: Use what you already have in your pantry and reduce food waste.
  • Stay Healthy: Access balanced meal suggestions and track your nutrition goals.
  • Boost Confidence: Follow easy, step-by-step recipes that help you level up your cooking skills.

Our Vision

We believe that healthy eating is the key to unlocking your full potential—academically, physically, and mentally. With NutrFed, we’re building a future where students are empowered to make choices that fuel their success, both in the classroom and beyond.
